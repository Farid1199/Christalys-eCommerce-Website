OMEGA De Ville Mens Co-Axial 39.5mm Automatic Watch

The ever-popular De Ville Prestige collection showcases timeless elegance and sophistication. This collection pays homage to De Ville's heritage, featuring the renowned 2500 calibre movement. The watch boasts a 39.5mm stainless steel case that seamlessly pairs with a black leather strap, adding a touch of refinement to its design.

WORLD-RENOWNED AESTHETIC

Commanding a large, loyal following around the globe, the De Ville Prestige collection is world-renowned for its pure, elegant aesthetic and truly timeless design.

CLASSIC DESIGN

Proving a minimal aesthetic can make maximum impact, this 39.5mm watch features a stainless steel case, paired with a classic black leather strap. The striking, two-zone silver-tone dial features Roman numerals at 3,6,9, and 12 o’clock, as well as a date window at 3 o’clock. Delivering flawless precision, the 2500 calibre movement is officially certified, has a 48-hour power reserve, and is water resistant to 3 bar (30 meters).
